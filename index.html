Our Work

Who We Are

Red Velvet Creative specializes in work that is strongly concept based and clean, simple designs created by organizing information and finding clever and aesthetically pleasing solutions. Our specialties include magazine work and trade conference branding, theatre designs and most recently, digital publications.

Selena Robleto

Selena Robleto was the lead designer at the Shakespeare Theatre Company where she met Annie before venturing into freelance, which led to starting Red Velvet Creative. She graduated from the Visual Communications program at the University of Delaware and has 15 years of experience in print and digital design. Selena loves highly conceptual art, typography and simple, clean designs. When she’s not busy being a geek or playing with her kids, she has been doing a drawing a day project, watching a lot of Fast and the Furious movies and seeking out food trucks. Selena has strong ties with the DC Creative Guild and formerly the Art Directors Club, because she supports and adores the DC design community.

Annie Hunt

Annie Hunt has been working in the world of publications since her high school and college newspaper days. In 2007 she moved to DC and started working at the Shakespeare Theatre Company as their communication projects manager. In 2009, she took the plunge, joining Selena and Red Velvet Creative and she hasn't looked back. Annie is a 2004 graduate of Loyola Marymount University where she majored in theatre, minored in history and was the editor in chief of the school paper. In 2006 she received her MA in public history from CSU Sacramento. She has brought her love of deadlines and creative thinking into the world of publications for a variety of nonprofits, theatres and other organizations. When she's not too busy, Annie loves to start her day with morning walks to her local coffee shop.
